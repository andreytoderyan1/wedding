## General Image Guidelines

### Style Requirements:
- **Home Page**: Dark theme, sophisticated, modern, tech-forward
- **Landing Page**: Bright, clean, minimalist, professional
- **Consistency**: All images should maintain professional quality
- **Aspect Ratios**: 
  - Hero backgrounds: 16:9 (1920x1080 or larger)
  - Service cards: 4:3 or 1:1 (800x800 or 1200x900)
  - Icons: 1:1 (512x512 or larger)

### Color Schemes:
- **Home Page**: Dark backgrounds (slate-900, slate-800), blue/cyan accents
- **Landing Page**: White/light backgrounds, blue accents, soft pastels

### Image Sources:
- **Free Stock Photos**: Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay
- **Search Terms**: Use the terms above with filters for "free for commercial use"
- **AI Generation**: Use ChatGPT/DALL-E with the prompts above

### Recommended Image Sizes:
- Hero backgrounds: 1920x1080px (or 2560x1440px for retina)
- Service card images: 800x600px or 1200x900px
- Icons: 512x512px or 1024x1024px
- Stats/feature images: 400x400px
